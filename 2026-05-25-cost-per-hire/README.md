# Cost-Per-Hire Calculator

A small CLI that reads recruiting data from a CSV and computes cost-per-hire (CPH) and average days-to-fill per role, plus totals across the whole org.

## Why I built it

Cost-per-hire is one of the workhorse metrics in any talent or HR strategy conversation — including consulting cases on operational efficiency. I wanted a tiny utility I could throw a CSV at to get a clean table, no spreadsheet wrestling.
